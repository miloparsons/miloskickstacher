    About

    The Movember Foundation is the only global charity focused solely on men's health, so this Movember I'm getting on board. I'm raising funds to tackle some of the biggest health issues faced by men: prostate cancer, testicular cancer, and poor mental health. Help me stop men dying too young.

    If this wasn't already enough of a reason to donate, I'm also giving you rewards for donating.

    "WHAT DO YOU MEAN?" I hear you ask. Well, a few years ago I had one of my most fun and successful Movember fundraising months ever when I gave people rewards for their amazing donations. I thought I'd step it up a notch this year though, and so I've made this Kickstarter-esque page to show you all of the rewards you could get!
